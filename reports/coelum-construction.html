1 Executive Summary
61/100
Needs Work

Coelum Construction has a solid local foundation in Tampa with a strong reputation as a woman-owned, family-owned licensed general contractor handling kitchens, baths, additions, and outdoor living projects. The homepage ranks decently for branded searches and shows up in local map results, but the site is leaving real visibility on the table when homeowners search non-branded terms like 'kitchen remodel Tampa' or 'general contractor South Tampa.' Most of the site lives on subpages built years ago that are missing meta descriptions, missing H1 headings, and use generic browser titles like just 'Coelum Construction'.

On the technical side, the site is on HTTPS with a working XML sitemap and a clean canonical setup, but image alt text is missing on roughly 44% of homepage images, the LocalBusiness / GeneralContractor schema isn't fully implemented, and there are no neighborhood-level service area pages (South Tampa, Carrollwood, Westchase, Brandon, etc.). Fixing the on-page basics on the existing service pages and adding location and project-type pages would unlock the bulk of the search opportunity in the next 60-90 days without a full rebuild.

7 Prioritized Action Plan
