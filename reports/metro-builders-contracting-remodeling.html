Metro Builders has been serving the Phoenix metro area for over 39 years, which is a tremendous asset for building trust with search engines and potential customers. However, your website is leaving significant opportunity on the table. Key technical issues — including a misconfigured Open Graph title that points to a Facebook URL, missing LocalBusiness schema markup, and very slow page load times — are holding your site back from ranking where it should.

The good news is that many of these fixes are straightforward. Your site already has decent internal linking and a solid meta description. By addressing the technical foundations, adding structured data, and creating targeted service area content, Metro Builders could see meaningful improvements in local search visibility within 60-90 days.
