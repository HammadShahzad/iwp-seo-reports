1 Executive Summary

Critical

Complete Carpet Care has a solid foundation as a family-owned business with a clear service offering and a growing reputation in the Boise area. Your website does include LocalBusiness schema markup and has a reasonable page structure with dedicated service pages for each offering. However, there are several areas where your site is falling behind local competitors in search visibility.

The biggest opportunities are around on-page optimization (missing meta descriptions, multiple H1 tags, missing image alt text), content depth (thin pages with limited keyword targeting), and local SEO (no dedicated service area pages for the cities you serve). Addressing these issues could significantly improve how often your business shows up when Boise-area homeowners search for carpet cleaning services.
