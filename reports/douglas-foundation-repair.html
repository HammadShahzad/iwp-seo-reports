Douglas Foundation Repair has a solid local reputation with A+ BBB rating and multiple Angie's List awards, but the website isn't fully leveraging that credibility online. The site has good bones — structured data for local search, dedicated service pages, and a clear service menu — but critical on-page SEO gaps are limiting visibility for the exact searches homeowners use when they need foundation help in Austin.

The biggest opportunities are around missing meta descriptions on key pages, slow page load times (7+ seconds), no dedicated city/neighborhood landing pages, and thin content on service pages that could rank for high-intent keywords. Addressing these issues could significantly increase the number of homeowners finding Douglas Foundation Repair through Google searches.
