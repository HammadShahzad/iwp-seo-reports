1 Executive Summary
34/100
Critical

MaK Construction's online presence has significant room for improvement. The main website at makdenver.com functions primarily as a landing page with minimal content — roughly 10 words of body text — which makes it nearly invisible to search engines. Splitting services across three separate domains (makdenver.com, makdenvercommercial.com, makresidential.com) dilutes your domain authority and confuses Google about which site to rank.

The good news: your brand messaging is clear, you have schema markup in place, and the site loads over HTTPS. But critical gaps in content depth, local SEO signals, internal linking, and page speed are holding you back from showing up when Denver homeowners and businesses search for contractors. Addressing these issues could dramatically improve your visibility in local search results.

6 Content Gap Recommendations

Topics and content types your competitors cover that you're missing.

Service area pages for Denver neighborhoods High Priority

Captures searches like 'contractor in Castle Rock' or 'home builder Castle Pines' — these are low competition and high intent

Recommended format: Individual landing pages for each service area (Castle Rock, Castle Pines, Denver Metro, Littleton, etc.)

Project portfolio with descriptions and photos High Priority

Homeowners and businesses want to see your work before calling — and Google rewards image-rich pages

Recommended format: Gallery page with case studies including scope, timeline, budget range, and before/after photos

Customer testimonials and reviews page High Priority

Social proof builds trust and helps with local pack rankings in Google

Recommended format: Dedicated testimonials page with Google review integration

Detailed service pages (kitchen remodel, bathroom, additions, commercial buildout) High Priority

Each service needs its own page to rank for specific search terms

Recommended format: Individual service pages with 500+ words, photos, pricing guidance, and CTAs

Educational blog content Medium Priority

Attracts homeowners researching projects — builds authority and drives organic traffic

Recommended format: Blog posts: 'Cost to remodel a kitchen in Denver 2026', 'How to choose a contractor', etc.

About Us page with team and license info Medium Priority

Builds trust, shows expertise, and helps E-E-A-T signals for Google

Recommended format: Team page with bios, licenses, insurance info, and company history
