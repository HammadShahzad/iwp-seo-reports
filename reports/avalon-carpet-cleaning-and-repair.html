1 Executive Summary
64/100
Needs Work

Avalon Carpet Cleaning and Repair has built a genuinely solid foundation — 10+ years of experience in the Treasure Valley, a dedicated service area page structure covering Boise, Meridian, Eagle, Kuna, Garden City, Caldwell, Star and Middleton, and a clean HTTPS site with canonical URLs and a working FAQ schema. Content depth is decent with roughly 1,000 words on most pages, and the internal linking between services and service areas is logical.

Where the site is leaving rankings on the table is in the fundamentals. The homepage is missing a meta description entirely, has a duplicate H1 tag, and a title tag that doesn't mention Boise — the single most valuable local search term. Every image on the homepage (12 out of 12) is missing alt text. There's no LocalBusiness schema, no Open Graph tags for social sharing, and no visible review/aggregateRating markup. Fixing these isn't a heavy lift — most can be done in a single afternoon — and together they would meaningfully improve how Avalon shows up for Boise-area carpet cleaning searches.

6 Content Gap Recommendations

Topics and content types your competitors cover that you're missing.

Pet stain & odor removal guide High Priority

High-intent searchers in Boise regularly look for pet-specific cleaning help, and Avalon offers odor removal but has no dedicated page for it

Recommended format: Dedicated service page + supporting blog post with before/after photos

Carpet stretching explained (what it is, when you need it) High Priority

Carpet stretching is one of Avalon's differentiators vs. competitors who only clean. A clear explainer captures informational search traffic that leads to bookings

Recommended format: Long-form pillar page with FAQ schema and internal links to booking

How often should you deep-clean your carpet? Medium Priority

Informational query with strong purchase intent downstream. Low competition in Boise results

Recommended format: Blog post, 800-1,200 words, include a printable maintenance calendar

Service area neighborhood pages (enriched) High Priority

Current service-area pages likely rank lower than they could because they may share boilerplate copy. Each needs unique local content

Recommended format: 400-600 unique words per page referencing local neighborhoods, HOAs, common flooring issues in that area

Customer reviews & case studies Medium Priority

No on-site review surface with schema markup means Google doesn't see aggregate rating, and prospects have to leave the site to verify credibility

Recommended format: Dedicated /reviews/ page pulling from Google, Facebook, and a handful of written case studies with photos

Commercial carpet cleaning offer Medium Priority

B2B searches ('office carpet cleaning Boise') have high commercial value and low competition. Not currently targeted

Recommended format: Dedicated commercial services page with industries served, after-hours availability, recurring service pricing
