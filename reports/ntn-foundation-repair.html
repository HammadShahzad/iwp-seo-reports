1 Executive Summary
56/100
Critical

NTN Foundation Repair has built a solid reputation over 20+ years serving Nashville, and the website does a good job introducing the core services and surfacing trust signals like BBB accreditation and 24/7 availability. The homepage content is substantial and the service lineup is clear, which is a strong starting point most smaller contractors haven't reached yet.

That said, the site is leaking visibility on exactly the searches Nashville homeowners are actually typing. A missing meta description, a Gmail-style contact email on most pages, no LocalBusiness schema, thin service pages for several repair types, and a lack of problem-focused blog content (cost guides, warning-sign articles) mean competitors like United Structural Systems and Olshan are capturing clicks that should be landing here. The fixes are straightforward and the upside is meaningful — most improvements can be rolled out in a few weeks.

7 Prioritized Action Plan
