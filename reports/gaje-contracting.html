1 Executive Summary
52/100
Critical

Gaje Contracting has a solid reputation in the Phoenix metro area with over a decade of foundation repair experience and ICRI membership. However, the website is leaving significant search visibility on the table. The homepage title tag is simply "Gaje Contracting" with no mention of foundation repair or Phoenix — a missed opportunity every time someone searches for help with their foundation.

Page load times are sluggish at nearly 7 seconds, and while the site does have LocalBusiness schema markup (a plus), it lacks city-specific landing pages, in-depth service content, and a blog strategy that could capture homeowners researching foundation problems. Competitors like Concrete Repairman have built out city pages across 9+ locations and maintain rich content that drives organic traffic.
