1 Executive Summary
52/100
Critical

3rd Story Architects has built an impressive 40-year track record in Arizona's architecture scene, but the website isn't working nearly as hard as the firm itself. The site loads slowly (over 10 seconds for a full page load with 124 resources), most portfolio images are missing descriptive alt text that would help Google understand your work, and there's no LocalBusiness schema markup — which means search engines don't clearly recognize you as a local Scottsdale architecture firm.

The good news: the site has solid bones. The internal link structure is well-organized across service categories, there's existing schema markup for Organization and WebPage types, and the content covers a good range of services. With targeted improvements to page speed, image optimization, local SEO signals, and dedicated service-area pages, 3rd Story could significantly increase visibility for high-intent searches like 'architect Scottsdale AZ' and 'custom home architect Phoenix'.

7 Prioritized Action Plan
Quick Wins do this week — under 2 hours each
  1. Add descriptive alt text to all images across the site — describe the project type, style, and location in each alt tag
  2. Implement LocalBusiness and Architect schema markup with NAP (name, address, phone) details
  3. Add self-referencing canonical tags to every page to prevent duplicate content issues
  4. Consolidate to a single H1 tag on the homepage with primary keyword focus
  5. Restructure title tags to lead with keywords: 'Scottsdale Architect | [Service] | 3rd Story'
  6. Enable lazy loading for all below-the-fold images to improve initial page load speed
  7. Submit XML sitemap to Google Search Console and verify indexing of all key pages
