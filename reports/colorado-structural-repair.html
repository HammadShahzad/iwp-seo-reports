1 Executive Summary
54/100
Critical

Colorado Structural Repair has built a solid reputation over 20+ years serving the Denver metro area, and the website reflects that credibility with BBB A+ accreditation and genuine customer testimonials. The site has good bones — HTTPS is properly configured, LocalBusiness schema markup is in place, and there's a logical service area page structure covering key Colorado cities.

However, several critical SEO gaps are limiting the site's visibility in search results. Missing meta descriptions across pages, a slow page load time of nearly 9 seconds, thin content on service pages, and a lack of FAQ schema are all leaving traffic on the table. Competitors like Pinnacle Structural Services have 19+ dedicated service pages compared to your handful, and Denver Foundation Solutions outranks with richer content and stronger on-page optimization. Addressing these gaps could significantly increase the number of homeowners finding you through Google.

Signs your Denver home has foundation problems High Priority

High-volume informational query that builds authority and captures homeowners early in their research journey.

Recommended format: Photo-heavy guide showing cracks, sticking doors, uneven floors with explanations and FAQ schema

Individual service pages for each repair method High Priority

Competitors have 11-19 dedicated service pages. Each service page targets unique keywords and captures specific search intent.

Before and after project gallery with descriptions Medium Priority

Visual proof of work quality. Photo galleries with descriptions also create indexable content for image search.

Comparing foundation repair methods Medium Priority

Homeowners searching for 'push piers vs helical piers' or 'carbon fiber vs wall anchors' need guidance.
