1 Executive Summary
64/100
Needs Work

Chapman Drainage has a solid foundation: 25+ years of family-owned reputation in Central Ohio, an A+ BBB rating, and decent existing technical setup with Yoast SEO and an SSL certificate. The site loads cleanly, images all have alt text, and the basic meta information is in place across most pages. That's a stronger starting point than most local foundation repair sites we review.

The biggest gaps are local SEO signals and content depth. The homepage is missing an H1 tag entirely, there's no LocalBusiness schema (which Google heavily uses for ranking local service businesses), and key terms like 'Columbus' and 'foundation repair' aren't appearing in the homepage title where they should. There's also a real opportunity to build out individual service pages for pier installation, bowed wall repair, and crack repair — right now a lot of services share one combined page, which makes it hard to rank for specific intent searches homeowners actually use.

7 Prioritized Action Plan
Quick Wins do this week — under 2 hours each
  1. Add an H1 tag to the homepage with the phrase 'Columbus Foundation Repair & Basement Waterproofing'
  2. Update homepage title tag to include 'Columbus' — e.g. 'Columbus Foundation Repair & Drainage Experts | Chapman Drainage'
  3. Update Foundation Repair page H1 from 'Repair Contractors in Columbus' to 'Foundation Repair Contractors in Columbus'
  4. Add LocalBusiness schema with full NAP (name, address, phone) — biggest local SEO lift available
  5. Add FAQ schema to existing service pages where you already answer common questions
  6. Add a clear NAP block (name, address, phone) in the footer of every page
Strategic Investments plan for this quarter
  1. Build dedicated service pages: pier installation, bowed wall repair, foundation crack repair, crawl space encapsulation, sump pump installation
  2. Launch 6–8 service area pages for Dublin, Hilliard, Westerville, Worthington, Powell, Upper Arlington, Grove City, Pickerington
  3. Start an educational blog targeting 'signs of foundation problems', 'foundation repair cost Columbus', 'how long does foundation repair take', 'cracks in walls causes'
  4. Build out a before/after project gallery with location, repair type, and photos for each project
  5. Create a cost guide hub page with pricing ranges by repair type — these pages convert exceptionally well
  6. Set up Google Business Profile posts on a regular cadence to keep local rankings fresh
