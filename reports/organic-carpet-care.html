7 Prioritized Action Plan
Quick Wins do this week — under 2 hours each
  1. Rewrite homepage title tag to include 'Organic Carpet Cleaning Portland OR'
  2. Add meta descriptions to all 12 pages (155 characters each with keywords)
  3. Add alt text to all images site-wide with descriptive, keyword-rich text
  4. Create and submit XML sitemap to Google Search Console
  5. Add LocalBusiness schema markup to every page with NAP data
  6. Compress all images to under 100KB and convert to WebP format

  5. Develop a commercial services page targeting offices, restaurants, and retail businesses
