K&M Steam Cleaning has been serving Austin since 1996 and has built a solid reputation with hundreds of positive reviews. Your website covers a wide range of services and includes good content depth on key pages like carpet cleaning and tile & grout cleaning. The site also has some structured data markup and a clear call-to-action strategy.

However, there are several areas where the site is leaving search visibility on the table. Missing meta descriptions on the homepage, inconsistent image alt text across pages, and the absence of LocalBusiness schema markup are limiting how well search engines understand and display your business. Additionally, competitors like Peace Frog and The Steam Team have stronger content strategies and local SEO signals that are helping them rank higher for key Austin-area searches.
