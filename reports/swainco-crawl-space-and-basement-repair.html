1 Executive Summary
64/100
Needs Work

SwainCo. has built a strong local reputation over 35+ years, and the foundation for a high-performing website is already here — clear service navigation, dedicated service area pages across the Indianapolis metro, and obvious trust signals like the BBB Torch Award and A+ rating. The site covers the right service categories (crawl space, basement waterproofing, foundation repair, exterior drainage) and already has the bones of a local SEO presence.

The bigger opportunity is on the content and technical side. Homepage and service page meta descriptions are missing or generic, schema markup (especially LocalBusiness and Service) isn't implemented, and foundation repair sub-services like pier installation, wall bracing, and crack repair share a single page rather than having their own dedicated, keyword-targeted pages. Homeowners searching Google for specific problems — cracked walls, sticking doors, sagging floors — land on competitor pages first because those competitors publish deeper problem-oriented content. Fixing these items should move you up meaningfully for city + service searches across central Indiana.
