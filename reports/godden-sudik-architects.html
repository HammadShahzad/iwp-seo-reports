1 Executive Summary

Godden | Sudik Architects has a strong brand foundation with 33+ years of experience and an award-winning portfolio. The website has solid basics in place — a keyword-rich title tag, a clear meta description, and HTTPS security. However, several critical technical and on-page issues are holding the site back from reaching potential clients searching for residential architects in the Denver metro area.

The most pressing concerns are extremely slow page load times (over 10 seconds), nearly half of all images missing descriptive alt text — a major issue for a visually-driven architecture firm — and the absence of LocalBusiness schema markup that would help Google connect your firm to local searches. Addressing these issues could significantly improve how often your work shows up when homeowners and developers search for architects in Denver.
